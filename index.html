Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our website and app development services, including timelines, costs, and post-launch support.

1. What services do you offer?

We specialize in custom website and app development, including UI/UX design, front-end and back-end development, e-commerce solutions, and ongoing maintenance and support.

2. How long does it take to develop a website or app?

The timeline varies depending on the project's complexity and requirements. Typically, a standard website may take 1-4 weeks, while more complex apps can take 3-6 months or longer.

3. What is the cost of developing a website or app?

The cost depends on various factors, such as the scope, design, features, and technology stack. After an initial consultation, we provide a customized quote tailored to your specific needs.

4. Do you offer post-launch support and maintenance?

Yes, we offer ongoing support and maintenance packages to ensure your website or app remains up-to-date, secure, and fully functional after launch.

5. Will my website or app be mobile-friendly?

Absolutely! We prioritize responsive design to ensure that your website or app looks and performs well on all devices, including smartphones, tablets, and desktops.
